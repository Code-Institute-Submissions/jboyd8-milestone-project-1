My Photography Journey

I bought my first DSLR when I was around 22. So this isn't something I have done all my life. A friend at the job I was working at the time took me out around Manchester one night, showing me all the beautiful images he could capture 2 minutes after leaving work. I was sold. I saved up for a few months and bought myself a Nikon. I quickly found a love for Landscape photography. There's something about being out in the countryside that you just can't beat. Nailing an image just tops things off. The reason I fell in love with it is because its an escape. When I'm out looking for a picture it's just me, my camera and the landscape. You can momentarily leave all your real world troubles behind.

Away from Photography

Outside of photography I work full time in the financial industry. I have been in the industry for just short of 11 years taking on various roles. I have a keen interest in Football. My team of choice, Hibernian FC. I have recently enrolled in a course to become a Full Stack Web Developer.
